# Interview Scheduling - User Guide

## For Employers

### How to Schedule an Interview

1. **Navigate to Employee Dashboard**
   - Log in as an employer
   - You'll see your dashboard with stats and applications

2. **Go to Applications Tab**
   - Click on "Applications" in the left sidebar
   - You'll see all applications received for your job postings

3. **Select a Candidate**
   - Find the candidate you want to interview
   - Click the "Schedule Interview" button

4. **Fill in Interview Details**
   - **Date & Time**: Select when the interview will take place
   - **Duration**: Choose how long the interview will be (30 min, 1 hour, etc.)
   - **Type**: Select "Video Call" (default)

5. **Generate Meeting Link**
   
   **Option A: Zoom Meeting**
   - Click the blue "Open Zoom" button
   - A Zoom meeting link will be automatically generated
   - The link will appear in the "Meeting Link" field
   
   **Option B: Google Meet**
   - Click the green "Create Meet" button
   - A Google Meet link will be automatically generated
   - The link will appear in the "Meeting Link" field
   
   **Option C: Custom Link**
   - Paste your own meeting link in the "Meeting Link" field
   - This can be any video conferencing platform

6. **Add Notes (Optional)**
   - Add any additional information for the candidate
   - Example: "Please prepare a coding challenge" or "Bring portfolio"

7. **Schedule the Interview**
   - Click the "Schedule Interview" button
   - The interview will be saved
   - An email notification will be sent to the candidate

### View Scheduled Interviews

1. **Go to Interviews Tab**
   - Click on "Interviews" in the left sidebar
   - You'll see all scheduled interviews

2. **Interview Information**
   - Candidate name and email
   - Job position
   - Date and time
   - Meeting link (click to join)
   - Status (Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled)

3. **Update Interview Status**
   - Use the dropdown to change status
   - Options: Scheduled, Completed, Cancelled

### Tips for Success

✅ **Schedule in Advance**
   - Give candidates at least 24-48 hours notice
   - This allows them to prepare properly

✅ **Test Your Link**
   - Click the meeting link to verify it works
   - Join a few minutes early to test audio/video

✅ **Add Clear Notes**
   - Tell candidates what to prepare
   - Mention if they need to share screen
   - Specify dress code if applicable

✅ **Send Reminders**
   - The system sends automatic email notifications
   - Consider sending a reminder 1 day before

## For Candidates

### Receiving Interview Invitations

1. **Email Notification**
   - You'll receive an email when an interview is scheduled
   - Email includes date, time, and meeting link

2. **View in Dashboard**
   - Log in to your candidate dashboard
   - Go to "My Applications" or "Interviews" section
   - You'll see all scheduled interviews

3. **Prepare for Interview**
   - Note the date and time
   - Test the meeting link before the interview
   - Read any notes from the employer

### Joining the Interview

1. **5 Minutes Before**
   - Click the meeting link in your email or dashboard
   - Test your camera and microphone
   - Ensure good lighting and quiet environment

2. **During Interview**
   - Be professional and prepared
   - Have your resume and portfolio ready
   - Take notes if needed

3. **After Interview**
   - Send a thank you email
   - Wait for feedback from employer
   - Check your dashboard for status updates

## Troubleshooting

### Meeting Link Not Working

**Problem:** Zoom or Google Meet link doesn't open

**Solutions:**
- Check if you have the Zoom app installed
- Try opening the link in a different browser
- Contact the employer for a new link
- Use the "Join from browser" option

### Didn't Receive Email

**Problem:** No email notification received

**Solutions:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify your email address in profile settings
- Check your dashboard for the interview details
- Contact the employer directly

### Wrong Date/Time

**Problem:** Interview scheduled at wrong time

**Solutions:**
- Contact the employer immediately
- Request a reschedule
- Check your timezone settings

### Technical Issues

**Problem:** Can't generate meeting link

**Solutions:**
- Try refreshing the page
- Use the "paste custom link" option
- Generate a link manually from Zoom/Google Meet
- Contact support if issue persists

## Best Practices

### For Employers

1. **Be Punctual**
   - Join the meeting on time
   - Start and end as scheduled

2. **Professional Setup**
   - Good lighting and audio
   - Quiet, professional background
   - Stable internet connection

3. **Clear Communication**
   - Explain interview format upfront
   - Set clear expectations
   - Provide feedback timeline

4. **Follow Up**
   - Update application status after interview
   - Send decision within promised timeframe
   - Provide constructive feedback

### For Candidates

1. **Prepare Thoroughly**
   - Research the company
   - Review job description
   - Prepare questions to ask

2. **Test Technology**
   - Test meeting link beforehand
   - Check camera and microphone
   - Have backup device ready

3. **Professional Appearance**
   - Dress appropriately
   - Clean, professional background
   - Good posture and eye contact

4. **Be Engaged**
   - Active listening
   - Ask thoughtful questions
   - Show enthusiasm
